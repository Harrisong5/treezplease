Our Mission

Plant one tree for every person on the planet

41 Million tress are cut down everyday.

Restore ecosystems and promote biodiversity in the most at risk areas

According to the World Conservation Body IUCN, more than 12,000 species are threatened with extinction.

Increase planet-wide carbon emission absoption

About 30 percent of carbon emissions from burning fossil fuels are taken in by forests, an effect called the terrestrial carbon sink.
